Dampfnudeln

Description

A German comfort food! A dessert typical for Germany and Austria. Super soft and fluffy, crispy bottom and filld with a fruity jam. Topped with sweet vanilla sauce. Vegan!

Ingredients

Ingredients for 5 dumplings:

  • Dough:
    • 250g of flour
    • 2 Tsp instand yeast
    • 2 Tbsp sugar
    • 3 Tbsp melted vegan butter (magerine)
    • 125ml lukewarm plant milk
  • Filling:
    • 1 Tbsp of jam for each dumpling
  • To steam:
    • 80ml of water
    • 2 Tbsp of vegan butter
    • a pinch of salt
  • Vanilla sauce
    • 125ml plant milk
    • 1 Tsp of instant vanilla pudding powder
    • 1 Tbsp suger

Steps

  1. Add flower, sugar and yeast to a bowl and mix thoroughly
  2. Pour in melted margarine and lukewarm milk
  3. Mix and knead the dough
  4. Let it rest for one hour
  5. Cut into five pieces and form little balls
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes
  7. Fill the buns with the jam (flatten the dough and fold it around the jam)
  8. Let it rest again for 10 minutes
  9. Heat up the water-magerine-salt mix in a pan
  10. Put the buns in and steam fry for 20 minutes
  11. Heat up all the ingredients for the sauce until thickened
  12. Mandatory enjoyment
