Kohlrabi Soup

Description

A simple recipe for a tasty vegan cream soup

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Kohlrabi
  • ca. 500 ml vegetable broth
  • 150ml of vegan cream alternative
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the kohlrabi
  2. Chop it into pieces
  3. Bring the broth to a boil
  4. Add the kohlrabi and cook until soft
  5. Puree it
  6. Stir in the cream
  7. Season with salt and pepper
  8. Enjoy! (Mandatory)
