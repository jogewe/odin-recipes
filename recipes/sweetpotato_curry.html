Sweet Potato Curry

Description

A delicious sweet potato curry with chickpeas for 2 people

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato
  • 1 green bell pepper
    • 1 onion, 1 clove of garlic, 15g of ginger
  • 2 tablespoons of sunflower oil
  • 2-3 teaspoons of curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 200ml of vegetable broth
  • 250g diced tomatoes (canned)
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 fresh tomatoes
  • 1 can of chickpeas (425ml)
  • 150g soy yogurt

Steps

  1. Peel and dice the potato and bell pepper
  2. Dice onion, garlic and ginger and sauté in hot oil untill translucent
  3. Sauté sweet potato and bell pepper briefly
  4. Season with curry powder
  5. Stir in tomato paste and sauté
  6. Pour in diced tomatoes and broth and bring to the boil
  7. Season to tase with salt and pepper
  8. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes
  9. Wash the tomatoes and cut into wedges.
  10. Rinse and drain the chickpeas
  11. Add both too the pan and simmer for another 8 minutes
  12. Season to taste with salt and pepper
  13. Serve with rice
